Creative Art Class

  • Wed 4:00pm - 5:00pm
  • 7yrs – 12yrs
  • 10 per week (£6 with concessions)

In the creative Art Classes each child will make their own professional portfolio.

The Portfolio is a great way of keeping all the work together and can be used in later years if one wants to go to Art College or pursue a career in Fashion or Art.

Contact Us

Arts Teacher

Hi, im Bernadette Fitzgerald, a creative arts support teacher. I teach the children to express themselves through arts and crafts with the use of many materials ie sewing drawing ,creative models, moulding and designing , with a different theme each week .i also allow them to have a space to express their emotions through all creative arts , painting , Clay , paper masche , designing jewellery , marbelling , creating dream catchers etc.

I teach them confidence building through helping them how to create and develop their own catwalk in which they get to design their own individual outfits for the live end of term show , also each week the children will be creating their own portfolio folders so they can reflect on their skills and talents and have magical memories to keep. With everything ive included i also design the uniform and merchendise for the academy.

Arts Support Volunteer

I'm Nicole Denny, one of the Creative Arts Support Volunteers. My background is in fashion - Production, Product Development and Technical. I've worked for brands such as Karen Millen, Whistles, Farrah, Itchy Scratchy Patchy, Craig Green, House of Holland (currently) & design my own brand.

My aim is to allow the children to be themselves & find aspects of themselves from thinking outside the box. To advise the "rules" of the creative process and have them decipher what that means to them and express it for themselves via the medium of art - drawing/ painting/ writing/ artistic communication. I'm looking forward to also working with Bernadette (& other volunteers) to collaborate with all of the FFA joint ventures.
