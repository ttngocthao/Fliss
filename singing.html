Singing Class

  • Tue 5:00pm - 6:00pm
  • 5yrs - 12yrs
  • 10 per week (£6 with concessions)

Singing is the act of producing musical sounds with the voice and augments regular speech by the use of both tonality and rhythm. Singing is a fun and very effective way to help us build up their confidence and self esteem.

Contact Us

Singing teacher

I’m Rosie Holloway, a dedicated singing, piano, guitar and songwriting teacher with 7 years of experience, and a lifetime of performances under my belt. I began to play instruments when I was 4 and come from a musical family.

I believe every child is musical and just needs the right environment to bring out their confidence, inner creativity and help them to express themselves which I believe is pivotal to healthy development and sets them up for life. There is nothing I love more than helping with this.

I will be teaching singing on Monday’s, and encouraging them to not only sing but write their own melodies and words, which, in my experience, is always the most rewarding for them.
