Street Dancing Teacher

Erduan Xhaferi is a Creative Director, Dancer, Choreographer & Movement Coach who has become established as a significant creative force in direction & choreography. ​

Highly respected for his vast knowledge of dance and movement, he is acknowledged for his innovative approach to creative movement in numerous mediums including Music, Fashion, Art, Theatre, Award Shows, Live Events, Television & Film. His analytical method and desire to push the boundaries further means his creativity & inspiration is ever evolving.

A gifted dancer known for his dance skills and experience Erduan introduced for the first time various tStreet & Hip Hop styles to dancer and also to appear on Albania mainstream media after appearing on the only televised season of Albania’s Got Talent show in 2010 making it until the Final live show. Erduan’s exposure on Albania’s Got Talent made him well known in Albania, raising his profile which resulted in working on various new projects. Throughout his career, Erduan has taught at numerous dance companies, Schools, Colleges, dance events and worked on many community projects with Councils & arts organisations in the UK and internationally.

He focus is to provide dancers and young people from all walks of life a creative platform to be Inspired.

In 2011, Erduan successfully formed and managed his own company Society Dance at the age of 25. Society Dance has become a very successful company in the UK and Internationally providing platforms for young people to express their talents using Street & Hip Hop as a tool inspire, motivate and educate young people of all ages.
