Our Founder

Jaime grew up in the local community being very well known by all families and friends in the area. She studied performing arts at Barbara Speakes Stage School in London from the age of 11. She then went onto college to achieve a diploma in performing arts.

At 19 years old she became a talent judge working alongside a unique talent management agency.

Jaime studied personal training at the YMCA in Southhall, giving her a degree in teaching exercise to music and fitness.

At 25 she then went on to study at Westminsters play center services for 7 years and trained to be a manager.

Jaime has combined all her knowledge and skills to help build Fliss Fame Academy for the local community helping to support local communities come together by bringing amazing opportunities to all young people from a wide variety of different grounds.

Combining the performing arts with sports and talent scouting, Jaime knew that there was a great need in deprived areas in west London where young people needed educational, stimulating, motivational activities and somewhere safe where they could go for after school and holidays.

We are raising £1,000 towards a years rent on a space, to continue supporting hundreds of deprived children in our local community.
