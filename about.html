Fliss Fame Academy

About Us

Fliss Fame Academy provides a variety of Performing arts and sporting classes for the young people within the local community of Maida Hill four days a week, and are located at The Stowe Club 258 Harrow Road W2, London. Stowe sessions run every Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday and Friday from 4pm – 6pm for ages 5 -16 years old for both boys and girls.

Our Purposes

  • Keep young people off the streets
  • Develop new life skills
  • Find hidden talents
  • Learn how to express themselves in a positive way

Partnerships
