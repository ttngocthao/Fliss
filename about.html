About Us

Fliss Fame Academy provides a variety of Performing arts and sporting classes for the young people within the local community of Maida Hill

Open: Mon - Wed - Thu

From: 4pm – 6:30pm

For: 5 -16 years

Our Purposes

  • Keep young people off the streets
  • Develop new life skills
  • Find hidden talents
  • Learn how to express themselves in a positive way
