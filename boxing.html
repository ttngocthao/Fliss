Boxing Coach

I am Roy Coleman, a professional football and sports coach for children’s schools and the fliss fame academy , with over 25 years experience and have won 10 bouts of boxing tournaments for major charity events all across London.

I have trained with the best and give all my students 100percent dedication. i believe all young people benefit from this amazing class , which teaches them self defence , self discipline , mind and body connections bringing a major awareness to their inner selves , it’s fun and very effective and is a great way to keep fit and healthy.

It’s a great way of building confidence and self esteem which I believe every child needs in life. It’s a very motivating and stimulating class that relieves so much stress and strain of the mind and has a high impact on emotional release. I love what I do and am a very passionate teacher.
