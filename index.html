About Us

The Fliss Fame Academy in West London, Maida Hill, is a community based performing arts, talent scouting and sports company, bringing a wide variety of amazing opportunities in all classes that are delivered to our young people of today.

Our main aims are to teach major life skills, develop individual goals, build confidence, self esteem, finding hidden talents, create and direct live performances and competitions all over London.

Read more...

Our Founder

Fliss Fame Academy was created by Ms Jaime Coombes way back in 2011!

Jaime has combined all her knowledge and skills to help build Fliss Fame Academy for the local community helping to support local communities come together by bringing amazing opportunities to all young people from a wide variety of different grounds.

Classes

Street Dancing

Street dancing originally comes from America where young people put together all sorts of dance routines.

This uses lots of different styles such as freestyle, contemporary, jazz , hip hop, ballet etc and put on live performances in the streets for all to see.

Drama

Drama is a discrete skill in itself (acting, theatre, refined skill) and therefore it is offered as a ‘subject’ in secondary school.

However Drama is also a tool which is flexible, versatile and applicable among a areas of curriculum.

Art and Design

In the creative Art and Design Classes each child will make their own professional portfolio.

The Portfolio is a great way of keeping all the work together and can be used in later years if one wants to go to Art College or pursue a career in Fashion or Art.

Singing

Singing is the act of producing musical sounds with the voice and augments regular speech by the use of both tonality and rhythm.

Singing is a fun and very effective way to help us build up their confidence and self esteem.

Football

Fliss Fame Academy’s Football sessions were created to support our young people.

It will help them to build up their confidence, self esteem, overcome shyness, improve mental, emotional and physical fitness.

Non Contact Boxing

Non Contact Boxing lessons are a fun and exciting way to give you not only a full-body workout, but also the basic skills needed in footwork, combinations, and self-defense.
